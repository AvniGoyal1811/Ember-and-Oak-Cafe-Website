Our Story

Welcome to Ember & Oak, where coffee is more than a drink; it’s an experience, a journey of flavours!. I’m Ojasvi Jacob, a passionate barista. My love for coffee began with the rich aroma wafting from my grandmother's kitchen each morning. Those moments inspired me to dive deep into the art of coffee and bring something truly special to life- my dream of opening a café where each cup tells a story. At Ember & Oak, we work closely with sustainable farms, carefully selecting beans to bring you a rich, authentic coffee experience. Every cup is crafted to create moments of comfort, connection, and joy.



Our Vision

Our vision at Ember & Oak is to be a welcoming haven where people can connect and savor exceptional coffee and tea. We believe in sustainable sourcing and the art of brewing, blending tradition with innovation in each cup. Whether you're here for a morning espresso or a calming tea, we aim to make every visit memorable and meaningful. Let's brew something unforgettable together.



Here’s to a community that gathers over great coffee, one cup at a time. Welcome to Ember & Oak— let’s brew something unforgettable together.
